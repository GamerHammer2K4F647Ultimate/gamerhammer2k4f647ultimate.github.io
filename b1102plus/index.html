How to insall

Since this is a jar mod, you have to install it manually.

1. extract mod.zip somewhere dosent really matter

2. go to %appdata%\\versions and then find b1.1_02 (if youre on betacraft, then open versions\b1.1_02.jar with 7zip or winrar or whatever), and open b1.1_02.jar

3. grab the mods files (the classes and the net folder) and drop it into the jar

4. delete META-INF in the jar

5. play the game with zooimg and some bugfixes lmao
